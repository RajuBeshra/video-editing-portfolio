// 01 Toolchain

The generative stack

Every tool below is in active weekly use — not a badge wall. This is the actual pipeline footage moves through, generation to grade.

Image gen NanoBanana 2 / Pro Character-consistent stills, meme-face + cinematic frame references for downstream video gen.
Video gen Google Flow (Veo 3.1) Primary cinematic + commercial video generation, scene-by-scene prompt direction.
Video gen Kling 2.6 / 3.0 Motion-heavy and product/commercial shots where Kling's physics reads more natural.
Image gen Omini Flash Fast-iteration stills for storyboard and shot-planning before committing to full renders.
Post / NLE Adobe Premiere Pro Full assembly, colour grade, sound design and delivery for cinematic and long-form work.
Post / NLE CapCut Pro Fast-cut, meme-heavy and vertical short-form edits — captions, beat-sync, trend formats.
